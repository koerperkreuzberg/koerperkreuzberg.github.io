Kōrper is a place of physical practice and the passion for human development. Our training is based on two foundational pillars: the development of strength & skills and the exploration of movement. You will move your body against resistance, through larger ranges of motion, lift weights, hang from rings and try new things.

Kōrper is suitable for everyone. For people of all ages and gen­ders, whether you already have an active lifestyle or not.

We offer guided personal training & group classes.

Your goals & your needs.
We help you embrace your own process and love every part of the way.

Move better & feel better.
Build muscle & lose weight.
Just being better.

At your time & your pace.
With your requirements & possibilities.

This is a group practice – cultivating an expe­rience of community and interaction. From body to body. From being taught to learning. We utilize the best tools from different disciplines such as gymnastics, dance and martial arts. We explore movement through play and improvisation, lift weights and work with our partners.

No dogmas & no trends.
We work with principles and a well thought out process that we continuously develop.
